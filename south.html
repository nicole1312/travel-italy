South Italy

◈ Molise, Campania, Puglia, Basilicata, Calabria, Sicily ◈

Southern Italy has some absolutely magnificent offerings including beautiful stretches of dramatic coastline, picture perfect beaches, charming coastal islands and a plethora of historic cities and towns. Possibly the best known region is the gorgeous Amalfi coast; this area on the Mediterranean coast is regarded as one of the most beautiful in Europe. Bustling Naples, Royal Palace of Caserta( the largest royal residence in the world), the dramatic ruins of Pompeii , the romantic Capri island, laidback Puglia and stuning beaches of Calabria, as well as up-and-coming agriturism help making Italy's less visited regions great places to explore. Just off the 'toe' of Italy's 'boot' sits Sicily , the largest of the Mediterranean islands and home to mighty Mt Etna, Europe's highest active volcano. Romans, Greeks, Arabs and others have all left their mark on this gorgeous island. With so much to offer, a trip to Southern Italy can be a true adventure. Discover more!
